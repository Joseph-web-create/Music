Rap Music

Rap music, a genre that originated in the African American communities in the 1970s, has grown to become a global phenomenon. Characterized by its rhythmic vocal style, rap music often features complex lyrics that address a variety of social, political, and personal themes. The genre has produced some of the most influential and best-selling artists in the music industry.
